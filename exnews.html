LeBron James Drops 40 Points at Age 40

On February 20, 2025, LeBron James defied age and expectations once again, scoring an incredible 40 points to carry the Los Angeles Lakers to a crucial 110-102 victory against the Portland Trail Blazers.

Not only did LeBron showcase his signature athleticism and leadership, but this performance also made him the first player in NBA history to record multiple 40-point games after turning 40, surpassing the legendary Michael Jordan.

Beyond the scoring, LeBron contributed 8 rebounds and led the team with his unmatched court vision and veteran savvy. Fans and analysts alike hailed this as one of the greatest performances of his career.

This historic night adds yet another chapter to LeBron's already legendary career and proves that age is just a number for The King.
